About

Gull Pavon is a data scientist and engineer with over a decade of experience driving insights and building intelligent systems for major enterprises. He has led data initiatives across Fortune 500 companies, delivering predictive models, real-time analytics pipelines, and AI-powered solutions that have impacted millions of users and driven measurable business outcomes.

His work spans machine learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and large-scale data infrastructure — from early-stage research to production systems handling billions of data points. He's built recommendation engines, fraud detection systems, customer intelligence platforms, and autonomous decision-making tools for organizations across finance, technology, and e-commerce.

Now, through Pavon & Co, he channels that enterprise-grade experience into building independent tools and products — applying the same rigor, architecture, and user-centric design that powers systems at scale, but with the speed and creativity of a solo builder.
